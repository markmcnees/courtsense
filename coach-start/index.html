Welcome to CourtSense

Getting started
with your app

Your team's app is set up and ready. Here is everything you need to log in and get your roster going. Takes about ten minutes.

1

Open your app

Your welcome email has your team's web address. It looks like courtsense.app/your-team. Open it in any phone or computer browser.

Tip: On your phone, add it to your home screen so it opens like an app. In your browser menu, choose "Add to Home Screen."
2

Log in as the coach

On the opening screen you will see a Coach and a Player button. Tap Coach, then enter your coach PIN.

Your coach PIN is in your welcome email. It is unique to your team. Keep it private; anyone with it can manage your roster. You can change it anytime under Manage.

The Player button is for your athletes. Once your roster is in, players sign in on their own side to see their info.

3

Add your players

Building your roster is the first thing to do. In the Players section, add each athlete with their name and grad year. You can fill in more as the season takes shape.

  • Add players one at a time from the Players section.
  • Name and graduation year are the essentials to start.
  • You can set jersey numbers and skill ratings later, after tryouts.
Have last season's roster in a spreadsheet? Hang onto it. We are adding a bulk import so you can bring returning players in at once. For now, adding your core group by hand gets you going in a few minutes.
4

Find your way around

Your app has four main areas along the bottom. Here is what each one is for.

Players

Your roster. Add athletes, view and edit their info, keep your team list current.

Practice

Plan and organize practices and practice groups for your sessions.

Duals

Your match days. Set lineups and score duals as they happen, courtside.

Manage

Team settings, your coach PIN, and roster tools all live here.

Need a hand?

Stuck on anything, or want something set up differently? Reply to your welcome email and we will help you out. We would rather you ask than stay stuck.
