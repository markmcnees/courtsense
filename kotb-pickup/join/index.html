What's TruVolley?

TruVolley is a public beach volleyball rating system. If you've played sanctioned tournaments (USAV, AVP Next, p1440, etc.), there's likely a profile under your name with a numeric rating.

Sharing your TruVolley name lets us seed your starting rating in this app close to where you actually play, so the first few games don't whiplash your standings.

Don't worry if you don't have one. Most players start at 1500 and the system figures it out fast.
