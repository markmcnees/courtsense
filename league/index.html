What You Get

Everything You Need to Run a League

One platform for beach and grass volleyball leagues. From the first registration through the final standings.

AI Schedule Generation
Round-robins, partner rotations, and court assignments built in seconds. The schedule respects your team count, court count, weeks, and rounds per night.
⚡
Live Scoring
Scores sync to every phone the moment they are entered. Captains and players see results live, no refresh needed.
🏆
Standings and Stats
Automatic leaderboards with wins, losses, set differential, and point differential. The math runs itself, all season long.
📈
Player Ratings
Glicko-2 skill ratings that follow players across leagues, seasons, and divisions. New players settle in quickly; veterans see steady progression.

Payment
Connect your existing Venmo, PayPal, or whatever payment method you already use. CourtSense surfaces your payment link to players and tracks who has paid. We never touch transactions.

Player Communications
Automated emails for registration, schedule changes, and reminders. Captain rosters and contact info stay current without you chasing them down.
