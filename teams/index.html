The Difference

Pre-Dual Lineup Transparency

Both coaches build their dual lineups in CourtSense and post them. When both are in, the lineups reveal to both teams at the same time. Move a player more than one court out of position, and the app asks why; the note goes to the other coach. A fair, transparent exchange that both programs can trust.

01
Both Coaches Post Privately
Build your dual lineup in CourtSense and post it. Until the other coach posts theirs, neither lineup is visible to either team.
02
Reveal at the Same Time
When both lineups are posted, the app reveals them to both teams in the same moment. No waiting, no watching, no advantage to whoever submits last.
03
Big Moves Get an Honest Note
Move a player more than one court from where they would be expected, and the app asks for a short reason. That note is shared with the other coach. Strategy stays yours. Integrity stays on the table.

The Full System

A Complete Team Management System

From practice pairings to season exports, every part of running a beach volleyball program in one place.

🤝
AI-Assisted Pairing
The app proposes balanced pairings using player skill, match history, side preference, and court level. Coaches stay in control and adjust as they see fit.
⚡
Live, Real-Time Scoring
Scores update every few seconds during a dual, so parents and fans can follow along from anywhere in the world while the match is happening.
🎯
AI-Assisted Training Plans for Player Goals
A player sets a goal, and the app uses player stats and skill data to generate a personalized training plan toward it. The coach reviews, edits, and approves every plan before the player sees it, so guidance always passes through the coach.
📊
Stats, Records, and Rankings
Season-long per-player stats with win-loss, point differential, and percentile rankings across the roster. The leaderboard runs itself.
📅
Schedule and Dual History
The full dual schedule plus a complete history of past matches with court-by-court results.
🏐
Practice Modes
Run scrimmages, exhibitions, and intra-squad scenarios in practice, not just official duals. Team-versus-team or Queen of the Beach.
🔍
Opponent Scouting
Keep a running scouting log on opposing programs. Player notes and pair history per opponent, with a memory of every prior meeting.
📤
Season Exports
Export the full season to Excel anytime. Stats, pairings, schedule, and standings in one workbook.
