Tap a player for full rating breakdown. One rating per name across leagues + pickup. Manage Ratings · Manage Players

How Ratings Work

Every player starts at 1500. Win games and your rating climbs; lose and it dips.

The number next to your rating is your RD (rating deviation). Lower RD means the system is more confident in your rating.

New players start with a high RD, so the system is still learning where they belong. As you play more, RD shrinks and your rating gets steadier.

Your rating moves by a capped amount each game, so one night will not swing it wildly. The full season is what settles your rating into an accurate picture.

Ratings persist across seasons forever. Subs do not earn rating changes.
