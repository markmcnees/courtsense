What CourtSense Community Is

Built For The Player

Teams are for coaches. Leagues are for directors. Community is for you, the person who just wants to play, get better, and find their people on the sand.

01

Your Game

Find open play, pickup games, and partners near you. See what is happening on the sand this week and get in on it.

02

Your Stats

One profile that follows you across every league, season, and pickup night. Your record, your rating, your progress, all in one place.

03

Your People

Connect with players at your level. Build a partner list, see who is playing, and grow your local beach volleyball circle.

Where We Are

What You Can Do Now

CourtSense Community is live in beta. Here is what works today, and what we are building next. Your feedback shapes the order.

Available now
  • Create your player profile and get a starting rating. Enter your TruVolley number to seed it.
  • See your dashboard: your rating, your rank on the leaderboard, and where you stand.
  • Find and RSVP to pickup games you qualify for.
  • Schedule your own pickup games.
Coming soon
  • In-app pickup games that count toward your rating, the count this game option.
  • Tournament partner finder and training partner finder.
  • Community discussion board.
The Vision

Every Player. Every Game. One Community.

CourtSense started on the courts of Tallahassee. The community side is how we bring every recreational player into one place, where your game, your stats, and your people all come together.
