QUICK START

Everything you need to get your program running.

The first five minutes

  1. Open your app. Your URL is courtsense.app/ followed by your school's name. We sent it to you.
  2. Tap COACH and enter your PIN. We sent that too.
  3. Go to Manage, then Import and Export. This is where you build your roster.
  4. Add players one at a time, or import your whole program from the spreadsheet template. Roster, skills, star drill times, and verticals, all in one file.
    Download the import template
  5. Tell your team the app URL and the player password. They pick their name and log in.

What your players do

  • They go to the same URL you use.
  • They tap PLAYER, pick their name from the list, and enter the password you gave them.
  • Their name only shows up after you add them to the roster.
  • Once they are in, they can see their stats, their skills, their rankings, and their goals. They can enter live scores on game day, scout opponents, and change their password.
  • Tell them to change the password the first time they log in.

What parents get

  • Add a parent's name and email to a player's record, and they get your announcements.
  • Under Manage, then Communicate, you send an announcement to Everyone, Players only, or Parents only. Players see it in the app. Parents get it by email.
  • Parents can also see the public fan page for scores and results. No login needed.
  • Parent accounts are coming. Until then, this is how you reach them.

The four tabs

PLAYERS
Your roster, stats, skills, practice groups, and player goals.
PRACTICE
Team analysis. See your weakest skills and build a practice around them.
DUALS
Your schedule, the lineup planner, live scoring, and dual history.
MANAGE
Add players, import and export, send announcements, track logistics.

FAQ

How do I get my roster in?

Two ways. Add players one at a time under Manage, Import and Export. Or download the roster template, fill it in, and import it. The import matches on name, so you can import again to update players without creating duplicates.

How do players log in?

They go to your app URL, tap PLAYER, pick their name, and enter the password. Their name appears only after you add them to the roster. Tell them to change the password once they are in.

What can parents see?

There is no parent login yet. Add a parent email to a player's record and they will get your announcements. Under Manage, then Communicate, you can send to Everyone, Players only, or Parents only. Parents can also view the public fan page. Parent accounts are coming.

Is my students' data safe?

We store no health or medical data of any kind. Physicals, ECGs, and eligibility run through your school's official FHSAA clearance platform, not through us. We collect the minimum: names, grad year, and parent contacts. Nothing about a player is public until the player and a parent choose to make it public.

What does it cost?

Nothing for the 2027 season. The only fee is feedback. Tell us what is broken, what is missing, and what you need, and we will build it.

Can I get my data out?

Any time. Manage, Import and Export, Export Excel. Your whole program comes out in one file. You are never locked in.
